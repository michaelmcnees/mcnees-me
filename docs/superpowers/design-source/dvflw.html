// NODE_05 :: ABOUT LORE DUMP

ABOUT

I'm an engineering manager at WeGoLook, a Crawford & Company subsidiary, where I lead a multi-functional team of frontend, backend, mobile, and cloud engineers. My path here wasn't traditional — I started in customer support at Sweetwater Sound helping studio engineers troubleshoot complex networked audio systems, fell in love with technology, and never looked back.

After certifications in digital control systems and a move to Sweetwater's IT team, I enrolled at Full Sail and got my BS in Web Design & Development. That kicked off a career that's taken me from Laravel apps and legacy WordPress to leading frontend architecture across React, TypeScript, and Next.js. Frontend is my sweet spot, but I've been going deeper into backend and cloud engineering lately.

These days I spend a lot of time looking for ways to make my team's work more efficient. Agentic engineering is one of the most interesting problems in our field right now — and our platform is in the middle of major migrations to React Native, Next.js, and Go.
