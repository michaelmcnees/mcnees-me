Honeydew is the digital record of a home — appliances, maintenance schedules, the quiet everyday things that get forgotten until they break. The other thing I care about is a transferable data model: when the house sells, the new owner inherits the history, so nothing resets to zero. Closed-source for now, headed toward SaaS.
